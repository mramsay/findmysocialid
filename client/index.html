What is my personal profile URL?

Your profile URL is the the URL you get to when you're landed on your profile page. It should look something like this:

  • https://www.facebook.com/johndoe
  • https://m.facebook.com/martha.stewart
  • https://vine.co/johndoe
  • https://www.instagram.com/justinbieber

    • Your personal profile does not look anything like these examples:

  • https://www.facebook.com
  • Sally Struthers
  • sally@struthers.com

    • Still struggling? If you are logged into any of your accounts in your current browser, you should be able to click the corresponding icon below and it will redirect you to the respective site where your profile page resides. Once you're there, copy the URL from your browser's address bar and come back here to try again.

    What is this all about?

    This tool helps you easily find your Facebook numeric personal ID for admins, social plugins and much more! For integrations of specific Facebook plugins, such as the "Like" button and "Like box" and others, Facebook requires that you know your numeric user ID. Unfortunately, this can be difficult to find, especially if you have a "vanity" personalized profile URL. If you can't find your ID, or don't know what it is and need it, just enter your full profile URL in the above form, and we can retrieve the ID from the code of your personal profile page.
